Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a private university located in Phagwara, Punjab, India. Established in 2005, LPU has grown to become one of the largest and most reputable private universities in India. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields, including engineering, management, law, and more. LPU is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections. The university has a diverse student body of over 30,000 students from across India and abroad, making it a vibrant and inclusive learning environment.
